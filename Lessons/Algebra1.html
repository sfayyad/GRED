Algebra 2: Line of Best Fit


A line of best fit (or "trend" line) is a straight line that best represents the data on a scatter plot. This line may pass through some of the points, none of the points, or all of the points. In the attached PDF, there are exercises on finding the line of best fit based off of real data collected by Greenhills.
